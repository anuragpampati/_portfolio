About Me

I'm a software engineer at Amazon Web Services in Seattle, working on WorkSpaces Core. My focus is making AI systems reliable in real environments — not research, not demos, but production systems that engineers depend on every day.

Earlier this year I designed and shipped ws-core-ops-helper — an end-to-end LLM agent on Claude and MCP that runs the oncall workflow for my team. It pulls tickets, sets up credentials, correlates metrics, traces logs, and posts a structured diagnosis — autonomously. The team went from 45-minute alarm investigations to 10 minutes.

Before Amazon I built retirement benefits platforms at NYSTRS and REST APIs at LTIMindtree. I hold an MS in Computer Science from Syracuse University (GPA 3.67).
